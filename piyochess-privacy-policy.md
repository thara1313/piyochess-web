# Pedly – Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-03-05_

Pedly ("the App") is developed and published by **Taito Harada** ("the Developer").  
This Privacy Policy explains what data is handled, why it is used, and how users can manage their data.

## 1. Data We Handle
Depending on your app usage and permissions, Pedly may handle:

- Account data (Firebase Auth UID, display name, email address, profile photo URL)
- Health data you authorize (such as steps, sleep-related values, and heart-rate-related values)
- Check-in data you enter (mood, energy, stress, memo text)
- Group and invite data (group name, membership, invite token, ranking records)
- Push notification token (for Firebase Cloud Messaging)
- Subscription and purchase-related data (product ID, purchase ID, verification data, plan status)
- Profile image files you upload (stored in Firebase Storage)

## 2. Why We Use Data
We use data to:

- Provide step tracking and health summary features
- Enable group ranking and family sharing features
- Deliver app notifications and reminders
- Manage subscription status and premium entitlement
- Improve continuity and personalization of AI partner responses
- Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot service issues

## 3. AI Feature Data Use
When you use AI partner features, selected inputs (for example note text, health summary, and optional profile context) are processed through server-side functions and may be sent to an external AI model provider (Google Gemini API) to generate responses.

## 4. Ads and Purchases
- The App may show ads through Google Mobile Ads.
- In-app subscriptions are processed through Apple In-App Purchase.
- Related identifiers and transaction data may be processed by those providers under their own policies.

## 5. Third-Party Services
Pedly uses services including:

- Firebase Authentication
- Cloud Firestore
- Cloud Functions
- Firebase Cloud Messaging
- Firebase Storage
- Google Mobile Ads
- Apple In-App Purchase
- HealthKit / Google Fit (when authorized by user)
- Google Gemini API (for AI response generation)

## 6. Sharing and Disclosure
- Data shared within your group is visible to members of that same group.
- The Developer does not sell personal data.
- Data may be disclosed if required by law or to protect service security and integrity.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion
- Data is retained for the period necessary to provide services.
- Users can request deletion by using in-app account deletion flow.
- Some records may remain for limited periods in backups, logs, or legal compliance contexts.

## 8. Your Choices
- You can control health permissions in OS settings and app settings.
- You can control push notification permissions in OS settings.
- You can update profile data inside the app.

## 9. Children’s Privacy
Pedly is intended for general audiences.  
If a parent or guardian believes data was handled inappropriately, please contact the Developer.

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect product changes or legal requirements.  
The latest version is published on this page.

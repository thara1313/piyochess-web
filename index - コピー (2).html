About PiyoChess

PiyoChess is a simple mini chess game played on a 5×7 board. It focuses on short, readable positions instead of full 8×8 chess. Players use hearts to start battles, and hearts slowly recover over time.

You can play against an AI opponent, clear courses, and unlock character stories as you progress. The game is built for mobile and designed to be easy to pick up and put down.

How to Play

  1. Open PiyoChess on your device.
  2. Use hearts to start a battle course.
  3. Move your pieces like in normal chess (simplified board).
  4. Protect your king while trying to checkmate the opponent.
  5. Clear stages to unlock new stories and characters.

FAQ

Q. Is PiyoChess free to play? A. Yes. The app is free to download. It contains advertisements and optional reward ads to get extra hearts.
Q. Do I need an internet connection? A. Basic gameplay works offline. However, ads and some features may require an internet connection.
Q. What data does the app collect? A. We do not collect personal information directly. AdMob may use device identifiers for ad serving and analytics (see Privacy Policy below).

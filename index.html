How to Play

  1. Open PiyoChess on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Select a battle course and start the match.
  3. Move pieces with chess rules adapted for the 5x7 board.
  4. Protect your king and pressure the opponent into checkmate.
  5. Clear stages to unlock new episodes and character scenes.
